Quick Start Guide

1. Get Connected

Install the Tailscale app from your store. Sign in once—that's all you need to do.

Download Tailscale

2. Activate Private Link

Open the Tailscale app and tap the switch to ON when you want to chat. When you're done, just tap it OFF.

This keeps our chat private and encrypted from Big Tech.

3. Open Chat

Once Tailscale is "ON," tap the button below to join the secure chat.

Launch Private Chat
